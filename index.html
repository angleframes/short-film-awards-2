Who is Sharankrishna?

Sharankrishna was a vibrant creative spirit whose life was anchored deeply in the world of independent filmmaking and visual storytelling. Cinema was an absolute passion, a canvas for honest thoughts, and an arena for boundless raw expression.

The Story Behind Our Logo

Rules & Guidelines

1. Runtime Duration

All project submissions must keep total run times strictly at or underneath a maximum runtime limit of 30 minutes including credits.

2. Eligibility & Timeline

Films must have been completed on or after January 1, 2024. Works-in-progress are not accepted.

3. Original Content & Copyright

Applicants must hold all clear intellectual rights, licenses, and permits regarding both music tracks and imagery materials utilized inside the submission.

4. Campus Track Requirements

Submissions for the student division require proof of active institutional enrollment (Student ID Card Scan upload validation).

5. Review Panel Verdicts

Decisions and evaluations handed down by the official judging board remain absolute, terminal, and unalterable.
