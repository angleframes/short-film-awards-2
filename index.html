About Angle Frames

Angle Frames stands as a premier creative collective and platform committed to fueling the fires of independent filmmaking. Founded with a vision to build bridges for raw, unadulterated storytelling, Angle Frames discovers, nurtures, and highlights ground-breaking visual concepts. By organizing top-tier competitive arenas, masterclasses, and project distributions, we give voice to young digital visionaries across the region.

About Jain University

Ranked consistently among India’s top educational institutions, JAIN (Deemed-to-be University) acts as a powerful catalyst for human development and progressive learning. The Kochi Campus emphasizes creative exploration and technical mastery, specifically within its creative and media programs. Through its collaboration on the Sharankrishna Short Film Awards, Jain University reinforces its institutional drive toward nurturing industry-ready artistic talents.

Who is Sharankrishna?

Sharankrishna was a vibrant creative spirit whose life was anchored deeply in the world of independent filmmaking and visual storytelling. Cinema was an absolute passion, a canvas for honest thoughts, and an arena for boundless raw expression.

The Story Behind Our Logo

The core visual structure integrates a gold-toned camera aperture frame enclosing geometric perspectives. The closing aperture signifies pinning down a transient slice of time into a permanent timeline.

Rules & Guidelines

1. Runtime Duration

All project submissions must keep total run times strictly at or underneath a maximum runtime limit of 30 minutes including credits.

2. Eligibility & Timeline

Films must have been completed on or after January 1, 2024. Works-in-progress are not accepted.

3. Original Content & Copyright

Applicants must hold all clear intellectual rights, licenses, and permits regarding both music tracks and imagery materials utilized inside the submission.

4. Campus Track Requirements

Submissions for the student division require proof of active institutional enrollment (Student ID Card Scan upload validation).

5. Review Panel Verdicts

Decisions and evaluations handed down by the official judging board remain absolute, terminal, and unalterable.
