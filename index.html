Who is Sharankrishna?

Sharankrishna was a vibrant creative spirit whose life was anchored deeply in the world of independent filmmaking and visual storytelling. To him, cinema was not merely a medium of entertainment, but an absolute passion, a canvas for honest thoughts, and an arena for boundless raw expression. Known for his profound artistic perspective and supportive nature toward fellow creators, he dreamed of elevating stories that resonated directly from the heart.

The Story Behind Our Logo

The core visual structure of the Sharankrishna insignia integrates a gold-toned camera aperture frame enclosing geometric perspectives. This layout is deeply symbolic: the closing aperture signifies pinning down a transient slice of time into a permanent timeline—capturing life precisely as Sharankrishna loved to do. The surrounding gold crown and star hierarchy honor his memory, establishing a golden standard meant to continuously spark, recognize, and sustain the hidden cinematic spark within independent campus and general filmmakers.
